Are you more likely to become victim to crime when it's raining?

Do more crimes happen during darker or stormy days, where criminals are able to hide in the shadows and elude justice?
Our team dove into millions of crime reports from 5 major cities across the continental U.S. to analyze whether crimes occur more frequently under certain weather conditions.
